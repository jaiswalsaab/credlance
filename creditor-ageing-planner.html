📋 How to Export from Tally

  • Recommended (Payables): Gateway of Tally → Display More Reports → Statements of Accounts → Outstandings → Payables → F6 (Age-wise) → F2 (set period end date) → Alt+E → Excel
  • Alternative (Bills Payable): Gateway → Display → Statements of Accounts → Outstandings → Ledger → Select Sundry Creditors group → Alt+E
  • Required columns: Party Name, Bill/Ref No., Bill Date, Due Date (if credit terms set), Bill Amount, Outstanding Amount
  • Standard Tally exports work without any column renaming — the app auto-detects headers
  • If Due Date is missing, ageing is computed from Bill Date
