💡 Module A format: The stock movement report should cover a 24-month period with monthly columns. Each row = one stock item. The app auto-detects columns named with month names (Apr-23, May-23 …) and sub-headers (Opening, Inward, Outward, Closing). If your export uses a single aggregated period instead of monthly columns, the seasonality tool will use flat averages. Modules B & C are item-wise registers with columns: Date, Item Name, Qty, Rate, Value.
